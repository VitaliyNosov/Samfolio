ABOUT ME

My real name is Artem Palamarchuk and Kyle Walker is the pseudonym. I am young creative, easy-going and ambitious graphic designer from Kyiv, Ukraine. I have been crazy about design since I opened Adobe Photoshop for the first time 2 years ago. Ever since then, I've been constantly trying to improve my skills. Design is my driving passion. Everything in my life is affected by it and life decisions are based upon it. I prefer to design cool websites and well thought graphic user interfaces. I also continue to learn and practice other useful things around graphic and web design. I like to live active life so away from my work I love travelling, riding a bicycle and swimming. Italian food and English football are also about me.
